PC Sorting Tool. Add your own data and images, then start sorting!

Click on the item you prefer from the two shown.

The item you click gains points, while the other loses points. Items are sorted by their final point totals.

You can add your own data by editing the data file.



Keyboard controls during sorting: H/LeftArrow (pick left) L/RightArrow (pick right) K/UpArrow (tie) J/DownArrow (undo) S (save progress).

Before sorting: S/Enter (start sorting) L (load progress).
